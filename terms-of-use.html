Tempo app is a soccer social media app where football fans can relate with each other. Our app has a forum designated for each football club. The user is entitled to pick one of the five clubs on the Tempo app and join the forum. The five clubs are; Arsenal, Manchester United, Chelsea, Real Madrid, and Barcelona.

After signing up, any club you choose would be tied to your profile. Note that you won't be able to see what is going on in other clubs or forums. However, we give a one-time opportunity to change your club from your profile and once you've used that privilege, you're stuck with any club you choose.

Tempo app also features a merch store where you can purchase merchandise. Another feature in the Tempo app is the news feed which enables every user to post pictures or videos of soccer matches or anything that is soccer-related. People can like and engage in others' posts. There is also a prediction page where the admin predicts a score and other users can tell their predictions and at the end, a poll is being done to figure out who won the poll.
