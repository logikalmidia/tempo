What is Tempo?

Tempo app is a soccer social media app where football fans can relate with each other. Our app has a forum designated for each football club where the user is entitled to pick one of the clubs on the Tempo app and join the forum.

These services do not condemn or criticize any soccer team or club. It is specifically to aid interaction between soccer fans.

How to use Tempo?

After signing up, any club chosen by the user would be tied to their profile. Note that you won't be able to see what is going on in other clubs or forums. It is locked to only members of that particular forum.

However, we give a one-time opportunity to change your club from your profile and once you've used that privilege, you're stuck with any club you choose.

Clubs/Forums

This feature accommodates fans of a particular club. The users cannot be in two forums at the same time and they cannot see what goes on in other forums as well. However, there is a one-time opportunity for users to change their forums for life.

Newsfeed

This features the general news on all matches and soccer-related information. Users can also post pictures or videos of soccer matches or anything that is soccer-related on the news feed.

Prediction page

The prediction page just like the name implies, is for users or fans to predict scores of a football match immediately after the admin drops a post. After the poll, the winners get gifts for predicting right.
